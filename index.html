Amazing Space Facts

Why is space completely silent?
Sound travels through vibrations in air, water, or other matter. Since space is almost a perfect vacuum with very few particles, there is nothing to carry sound waves. That's why astronauts communicate using radio signals instead of speaking directly.
What would happen if you fell into a black hole?
According to current scientific understanding, the immense gravity near a black hole would stretch your body due to the difference in gravitational pull between your head and feet. This effect is called "spaghettification." Exactly what happens beyond the event horizon remains unknown.
How long does sunlight take to reach Earth?
Sunlight takes about 8 minutes and 20 seconds to travel the 150 million kilometers (93 million miles) from the Sun to Earth. This means when you look at the Sun, you're seeing it as it was over eight minutes ago.
Can it actually rain diamonds on other planets?
Scientists believe that on planets like Neptune and Uranus, extremely high pressures and temperatures may compress carbon into diamonds. These diamonds could sink through the planet's interior like rain, although this has not been directly observed.
